About Hotels.ng

Our team at Hotels.ng saw the problems of neglect and lack of attention the tourist section of Nigeria faced. And as students of Alx SE Africa, we knew we had the technical know-how to help improve the online experience of prospective tourists. Hence, Hotels.ng was born

Hotels.ng was inspired from a desire to bridge the gap between Hotels and their guests, a need to help tourists find a place to rest in an unfamiliar country.
